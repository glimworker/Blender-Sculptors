Learning Tips

  • Start with beginner tasks - they build your foundation
  • Save your work often (Ctrl+S or Cmd+S)
  • Take breaks every 30 minutes
  • Don't be afraid to go backwards - review easier tasks if needed
  • Help your classmates - teaching others helps you learn
  • Complete at least 2-3 tasks from each level
  • Mistakes are part of learning - use Ctrl+Z and try again!

Remember: Every expert was once a beginner. Have fun and be creative!
