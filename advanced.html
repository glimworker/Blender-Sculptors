Task 7: Castle in a Hill! 🏰

Architectural Sculpting

🎯 Your Mission:

Create a mysterious castle emerging from a hillside! Add towers, windows, and magical details to make it look like a wizard's home.

🔧 Tools You'll Learn:

  • Building architectural shapes
  • Creating stone textures
  • Making natural rock forms
  • Combining smooth and hard surfaces

📝 Step by Step:

  1. Shape the basic hill form
  2. Mark where the castle will emerge
  3. Create the main castle walls
  4. Add towers and battlements
  5. Make windows and doors
  6. Create stone textures
  7. Add magical details and finishing touches

💡 Hot Tips!

  • Mix rough rock with smooth castle walls
  • Think about where the entrance should be
  • Add cracks and wear to make it look old
  • Remember castles aren't perfect - add character!

🌟 Extra Challenges:

  • Add a secret entrance
  • Create magical floating elements
  • Make it look ancient and mysterious

✨ Show & Tell:

When you're done, be ready to:

  • Show how you blended castle and hill
  • Point out your favorite magical detail
  • Tell us who might live in your castle

Advanced Keyboard Shortcuts

Complex Operations:

  • Ctrl+R: Loop cut (essential for detail)
  • Ctrl+B: Bevel edges
  • Ctrl+J: Join objects
  • P: Separate selection
  • Alt+D: Linked duplicate
  • Ctrl+A: Apply modifiers

Precision Work:

  • Shift (hold): Precise movements
  • Ctrl (hold): Snap to grid
  • Alt+Click: Select loop
  • K: Knife tool
  • I: Inset faces
  • O: Proportional editing
