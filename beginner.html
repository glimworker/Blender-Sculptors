Before You Begin

Essential Shortcuts to Remember:

  • Ctrl+Z (Cmd+Z on Mac): Undo
  • Tab: Switch between modes
  • Middle Mouse Button: Rotate view
  • Shift+A: Add new objects

Task 1: Make a Funny Face! 😄

Your First Blender Adventure

🎯 Your Mission:

Create a super silly face that makes people laugh! Think big nose, funny ears, crazy eyes - whatever makes you giggle!

🔧 Tools You'll Learn:

  • Moving around in Blender (like a video game!)
  • Making basic shapes (spheres, cubes)
  • Sculpting tools (like playing with clay)
  • Adding fun details

📝 Step by Step:

  1. Start Blender and delete the starting cube (right click, delete)
  2. Add a sphere for the head (Add > Mesh > UV Sphere)
  3. Go into Sculpt Mode (click 'Sculpt' at the top)
  4. Use the Draw tool to pull out a nose
  5. Make funny cheeks and a chin
  6. Add big eyebrows and a silly mouth
  7. Make the ears however you like!

💡 Hot Tips!

  • Save your work often! (press Save or Ctrl+S)
  • If you make a mistake, press Ctrl+Z to undo
  • Take breaks every 30 minutes
  • Ask friends for help if you get stuck

🌟 Extra Challenges:

  • Add crazy hair
  • Make it look like your favorite cartoon character
  • Give it face paint or markings

✨ Show & Tell:

When you're done, be ready to:

  • Show your favorite part of the face
  • Tell everyone how you made it
  • Share one cool trick you learned

Task 2: Create a Cool Mask! 🎭

Symmetry and Sculpting

🎯 Your Mission:

Design an awesome mask that could be worn at a party! Make it symmetrical (same on both sides) and add cool patterns and decorations.

🔧 Tools You'll Learn:

  • Mirror modifier (makes things symmetrical)
  • Basic sculpting tools
  • Making patterns and designs
  • Adding surface details

📝 Step by Step:

  1. Start with a flat rounded shape (squish a sphere)
  2. Add the mirror modifier (to make both sides match)
  3. Pull out the basic mask shape
  4. Create eye holes
  5. Add decorative patterns around the edges
  6. Make surface details like scales or swirls
  7. Add final touches like a nose or horns

💡 Hot Tips!

  • Work on one side - the mirror tool copies everything!
  • Save your work after each major change
  • Use the smooth tool to fix rough spots
  • Think about where the eyes will look best

🌟 Extra Challenges:

  • Add animal features to your mask
  • Create a pattern that repeats around the edges
  • Make it look like it's made of metal or wood

✨ Show & Tell:

When you're done, be ready to:

  • Show how you used the mirror tool
  • Point out your favorite pattern
  • Explain how you made your best detail

Task 4: Cartoon Animal Head! 🦁

Character Expression

🎯 Your Mission:

Create a fun, expressive cartoon animal head! Choose any animal you like and give it lots of personality - make it happy, grumpy, excited, or silly!

🔧 Tools You'll Learn:

  • Creating expressive features
  • Working with proportions
  • Making exaggerated shapes
  • Adding character details

📝 Step by Step:

  1. Choose your animal and its expression
  2. Create the basic head shape
  3. Add the main features (eyes, nose, mouth)
  4. Shape the expression
  5. Add defining features (ears, horns, etc.)
  6. Create surface details
  7. Make final adjustments to the expression

💡 Hot Tips!

  • Bigger eyes make it more cartoon-like
  • Exaggerate the main features
  • Think about what makes your animal recognizable
  • Use reference pictures of cartoon animals

🌟 Extra Challenges:

  • Add fun accessories (hat, glasses, etc.)
  • Mix features from different animals
  • Create an extreme expression

✨ Show & Tell:

When you're done, be ready to:

  • Tell us what animal you chose and why
  • Explain the expression you gave it
  • Show how you made it cartoon-style

Task 13: Create a Space Rock! ☄️

Alien Asteroid Design

🎯 Your Mission:

Create a mysterious rock from outer space! Add alien crystals, strange metals, or weird glowing stuff that's definitely not from Earth!

🔧 Tools You'll Learn:

  • Making rough rock shapes
  • Creating crystal formations
  • Adding alien textures
  • Building strange patterns

📝 Step by Step:

  1. Form the basic rock shape
  2. Add impact craters or holes
  3. Create alien crystal growths
  4. Make weird surface patterns
  5. Add glowing areas
  6. Include mysterious details
  7. Polish with space dust effects

💡 Hot Tips!

  • Mix rough and smooth areas
  • Add details that look alien
  • Think about where it came from
  • Make it look mysterious

🌟 Extra Challenges:

  • Create strange energy effects
  • Add floating pieces
  • Make parts that look like alien metal

✨ Show & Tell:

When you're done, be ready to:

  • Tell us where your rock came from
  • Show its weirdest feature
  • Explain what makes it alien

Essential Keyboard Shortcuts for Beginners

Must Know:

  • Ctrl+S: Save
  • Ctrl+Z: Undo
  • Tab: Switch modes
  • G: Move
  • R: Rotate
  • S: Scale

Navigation:

  • Middle Mouse: Rotate view
  • Shift+Middle Mouse: Pan
  • Mouse Wheel: Zoom
  • Numpad 1: Front view
  • Numpad 7: Top view
